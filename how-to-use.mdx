## How to Lend

Learn how to lend your assets on PayStream and start earning yield.

- You cannot borrow in the market that you have lended and vice versa; i.e., you cannot be a lender and borrower in the same market.
- You cannot withdraw for seven days after you lend. Instantaneous withdrawals will be rolled out in the future.

## How to Borrow

Understand the process of borrowing assets using PayStream.

- To borrow, you need to put up collateral.
- You must lend in a different market first to borrow in another market. After you put some amount in a different market as collateral, you can borrow from another market.
- You cannot borrow in the market where you are a lender.
